NEAR BY SHOPPING MALLS

AMERICAN DREAM MALL

The American Dream Mall, located in East Rutherford, New Jersey, is a shopper’s paradise. This massive retail destination spans over 3 million square feet, offering an extensive selection of shops and boutiques catering to every taste and style. From high-end fashion to home decor and electronics, the American Dream Mall has it all. Whether you’re seeking the latest trends or timeless classics, you’ll find an unparalleled shopping experience at this iconic mall.

HUDSON YARDS MALL

Hudson Yards Mall, located in the heart of New York City, is a premier shopping destination for discerning shoppers. With its sleek and modern design, the mall offers a curated selection of luxury boutiques, high-end fashion brands, and cutting-edge technology stores. From designer clothing to fine jewelry and the latest gadgets, Hudson Yards Mall caters to those with a taste for the finer things in life. The mall’s stunning architecture and vibrant atmosphere create a shopping experience like no other in the city.

GARDEN STATE MALL

Garden State Plaza, situated in Paramus, New Jersey, is a shopper’s paradise with a rich history of offering unparalleled retail experiences. Boasting over two million square feet of shopping space, it’s one of the largest malls on the East Coast. With a diverse array of stores ranging from luxury brands to popular retailers, Garden State Plaza caters to every shopper’ s needs. Its convenient location and extensive dining options make it a go-to destination for both locals and visitors.

WOODBURY COMMON PREMIUM OUTLETS

Woodbury Common Premium Outlets, nestled in Central Valley, New York, is a shopping haven for savvy shoppers seeking incredible deals on premium brands. With more than 250 stores offering discounts on high-end fashion, accessories, home goods, and more, it’s a shopper’s dream come true. The picturesque outdoor setting adds to the charm, making strolling through this outlet center a delightful experience. If you’re in search of designer bargains and exceptional savings, Woodbury Common Premium Outlets is the ultimate destination for a day of retail therapy.

THE MALL AT SHORT HILLS

The Malls at Short Hills, located in Short Hills, New Jersey, is the epitome of upscale shopping. This prestigious mall features an exquisite selection of luxury boutiques and high-end retailers, making it a go-to destination for those seeking the finest in fashion, accessories, and more. With its elegant ambiance and exclusive offerings, it’s no wonder that The Malls at Short Hills has earned a reputation as one of the premier shopping destinations on the East Coast. Whether you’re indulging in a shopping spree or enjoying a gourmet meal, this upscale mall promises a truly luxurious and memorable experience.
